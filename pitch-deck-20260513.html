The Problem

Prediction markets work.
Four things have always been missing.

No permissionless creation

Platforms decide what markets exist. You don't.

Polymarket curates every market. A company can't list a prediction on their own product launch. An analyst can't monetize their thesis. A user can't trade their hot take. Market creation is gatekept.

No position privacy

Every bet is visible on-chain the moment you place it.

Whales see your position and front-run it. Informed capital stays out. Prices reflect noise, not conviction.

No autonomous resolution

Someone always has to decide — a human, a committee, a multisig.

Disputes take days. Votes are gameable. No record of why a decision was made. No accountability when they get it wrong.

No protocol liquidity

Every new market launches empty and waits for human LPs.

Liquidity providers don't show up for long-tail markets. Most markets die at 50/50 with zero volume.

Polymarket does $1B+ in monthly volume — and none of these four exist there.

The Product

Prediction markets already work.
Prophet makes them autonomous.

How prediction markets work today

1 · Someone creates a question

"Will BTC exceed $100K by end of 2025?"

2 · People bet YES or NO with real money

Prices move between $0 and $1, reflecting collective probability.

3 · At deadline, an outcome is determined

A person, committee, or data feed decides what happened.

4 · Winners are paid out

Correct bettors redeem shares at $1.00. Losers get $0.00.

Polymarket does $1B+ monthly on this model. It works.

What Prophet adds — three things no prediction market does today

NEW

Sealed positions

Your bet direction is encrypted client-side before it ever hits the chain. Nobody — not even the protocol — can see which way you bet until the market resolves. Simultaneously for all participants.

NEW

AI oracle

No committee. No vote. At deadline, an autonomous agent calls 0G Compute, reasons about the outcome with real evidence, and posts a verifiable verdict on-chain. Full reasoning stored permanently on 0G Storage.

NEW

Autonomous liquidity

A protocol-owned pool seeds every market the moment it's created. A market maker agent manages reserves 24/7. No human LP needed. Trading starts immediately on every market.

Architecture

Five contracts. Two autonomous agents.
One complete market lifecycle.

  1. Create — User submits a question → 0G Compute validates it → ProphetFactory deploys a MarketContract → metadata written to 0G Storage
  2. Seed — Market maker agent reads LiquidityPool → allocates USDT to AMM reserves → YES and NO shares are immediately tradable at opening price
  3. Trade — Users buy/sell YES and NO shares → AMM price adjusts with every trade → commitments encrypted in PositionVault (direction hidden until resolution)
  4. Resolve — Deadline passes → oracle agent calls 0G Compute → full reasoning written to 0G Storage → verdict posted on-chain → 24h challenge window → finalizeResolution()
  5. Settle — All positions revealed simultaneously → PayoutDistributor sends USDT to winners → pool liquidity returned → protocol fees distributed

Why 0G Labs

We don't run on 0G.
We require 0G to exist.

0G Compute

Decentralised AI inference. Censorship-resistant. TEE-attested.

SDK: @0glabs/0g-serving-broker

Oracle calls broker.verifyService() on every inference — refuses to post if TEE attestation fails. Hardcoded to temperature: 0.1 for deterministic verdicts.

Without it: Every market freezes at deadline. No resolution. User funds locked forever.

0G Storage

Permanent oracle audit trails. Verifiable by anyone. Economically viable.

SDK: @0gfoundation/0g-storage-ts-sdk

Uses turbo indexer (standard returns 503 on testnet). MemData upload → Merkle root hash committed on-chain. Read-after-write checksum verified on every write.

Without it: verdictReasoningHash on-chain points to nothing. The verifiability claim is a lie.

0G Chain

11,000 TPS. Sub-second finality. Cents per transaction.

Stack: ethers v6 · viem · wagmi · Chain ID 16602

Oracle submits 3–5 txs per market resolution. At Ethereum L1 prices: $50–$200 per market. On 0G: cents. The economics of a per-market AI oracle only work here.

Without it: The entire protocol. No state. No settlement. Not transferable at any other chain's gas structure.
